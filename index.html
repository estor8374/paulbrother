About Paul Brothers

Traditional Pottery Since 1995

Paul Brothers has been crafting exquisite earthenware products in Ultam Pal for over 25 years. Our journey began with a simple passion for traditional pottery and has grown into a respected business known for quality craftsmanship.

Using locally sourced clay and time-honored techniques, we create functional and decorative pieces that bring natural beauty into your home. Each item is handcrafted with care, ensuring unique character and durability.

Our mitti ke bartan are made without any chemicals or artificial glazes, making them perfect for healthy cooking and water storage.

Location: Ultam Pal, Jharkhand
